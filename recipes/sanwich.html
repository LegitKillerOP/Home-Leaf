Sandwich Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 6 slices of turkey or chicken
  • 4 slices of cheese (cheddar, Swiss, or your choice)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Red onion slices
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Spread mayonnaise on two slices of bread and mustard on the other two slices.
  2. Layer turkey or chicken, cheese, lettuce, tomato slices, and red onion slices on the bread slices.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Top with the remaining slices of bread to form sandwiches.
  5. Press down gently and cut each sandwich in half diagonally.
  6. Serve and enjoy your delicious sandwich!
