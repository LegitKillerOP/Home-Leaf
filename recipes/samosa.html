Samosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup ghee or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled and chopped mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, and green beans)
  • 1 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, ghee (or oil), ajwain, and salt. Knead into a smooth dough using water.
  2. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add grated ginger and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add boiled and mashed potatoes, mixed vegetables, turmeric powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  5. Stir in chopped cilantro and let the mixture cool.
  6. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin oval shape.
  7. Cut the oval in half to form two semi-circles.
  8. Take one semi-circle, wet the edges, and form a cone shape by sealing the edges.
  9. Fill the cone with the potato mixture and seal the open edge to form a triangular shape.
  10. Heat oil for frying and deep-fry the samosas until golden brown.
  11. Remove from the oil and place on paper towels to absorb excess oil.
  12. Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice.
