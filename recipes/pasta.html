Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g pasta (of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and black pepper. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce and coat evenly.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
