Shahi Paneer Recipe

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 1 cup onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup tomato, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cashews, soaked
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 1/4 cup ghee or butter
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pan, heat ghee or butter and sauté finely chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  3. Blend soaked cashews into a smooth paste and add it to the pan.
  4. Stir in turmeric powder, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until the oil separates.
  5. Add tomato puree and cook until the masala is well-cooked and oil starts to separate.
  6. Add cubed paneer and mix well. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Pour fresh cream and mix gently. Adjust salt according to taste.
  8. Crush kasuri methi between your palms and sprinkle over the shahi paneer.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot.
