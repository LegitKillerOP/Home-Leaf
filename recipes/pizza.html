Cheese Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza stone or a baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese on top.
  6. Season with dried oregano, garlic powder, and red pepper flakes (if using).
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  9. Serve and enjoy your delicious homemade cheese pizza!
